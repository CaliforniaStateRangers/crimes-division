Frequently Asked Questions

FAQS

What is the hour requirement for the Crimes Division?

In the Crimes Division, we require that you clock a minimum of 3 hours per week as it is one of the best subdivisions within the CSR. However, to get promoted within CD, you will need to clock more hours. For more information DM CD Command.

What rank can I join CD?

In order to be elligble for the Crimes Division, you must achieve the rank of Corporal I+ within the California State Rangers.

What rank can I recurit people into CD?

To unlock the Recruiting ability, you will need to achieve the rank of Senior Operative within the Crimes Division.
